# TheDagger
Iron Dagger: Quick, Effective and Simple I think you'll all agree with me that combat in Minecraft can be pretty awkward to get the feel of and it becomes more awkward as time goes on. If you wish it had more speed and precision to it then here you have it. This is an Iron Dagger for Minecraft Java 1.20.1 using Forge and, true to the name, is speedy and simple to use without the bulk of a giant sword.

I also created using MCreator so it does not have tons of random fantasy stuff and will only be dropped into your game. It is suitable for someone who would like a more rogue like ability or the convenience of having a small backup weapon available in their survival world.

The Iron Dagger: It stands out because of it's speed- you wont be slaying groups of mobs with the Iron Dagger but for when you're just whacking away at one or two creatures or have an emergency while your main tool is about to snap then this weapon will surely help with small, fast hits. Small, sharp and made for up close damage. Made of nothing but pure iron, so no enchantments or gems. Has quick single hit attacks. Works with forge's new 1.20.1 combat perfectly.

Crafting: You do not need to venture across the entire world in search of rare drops or to collect lots of items just for one sword. Just 4 iron ingots and 2 sticks can make this dagger which should work well with the price considering that the costs are less than that of a sword, which seems fair.

Extra's: Version: 1.20.1 Loader: Forge Mod Type: "Vanilla plus". These are mods which are extremely simple, very light and should not get in the way of a massively overhaul mod. This mod only includes the dagger.

The dagger is for those who need a simple tool that adds a new weapon into the game right from the very start. The knife could fit well into a stealth based world or it could simply be another addition to the weapon stand in your home base. It does exactly what it says.
